Bean Bag

Objective: Create a sell-able houseware product that inspires sustainability.

Project Members: August Majtenyi & Leila Akberdin


Research

We conducted informal interviews and used make-tool kits of clay and construction paper to learn about the characteristics of an ideal bag. We discovered that the most important qualities are comfort, durability, and functionality

Process

At the beginning of this project, I had no experience with sewing machines. My project partner taught me how. I now consider sewing to be a valuable skill in my repertoire.



The material we used was very prone to shedding. We experimented with a biodegradable coating that significantly reduced this.



Final

The bag has adjustable straps that can also transform into a backpack
