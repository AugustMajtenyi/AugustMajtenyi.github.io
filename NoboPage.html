Nobo

Objective: To create a product/service that improves the user experience of refueling electric vehicles.

Project Members: August Majtenyi & Shijie Li


Research

Our Research helped demonstrate that electric cars are rapidly growing in the U.S. market due to their environmental benefits. Also, the most frustrating aspects of public charging is finding a location and having malfunctions in the product.

Process

Final Renders

The nobo robot was designed to locate your car in the parking garage and wirelessly charge it while away.


Nobo is the epitome of convenience for the consumer, and it's modular system allows for easy repair on broken units.
