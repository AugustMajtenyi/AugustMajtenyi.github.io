Circuit Soccer

Overview: Circuit Soccer is a turn-based game that combines strategy and chance.

Players: 2

Estimated Play-Time: 20 minutes

Objective: First to score 3 points wins!


Setup

Each player organizes their six pieces on their respective side of the board.
(A maximum of 2 players are allowed in each team's own Goalie Box)

The Ball is placed in the middle of the board.

Players roll for who goes first.

Rules

Players take turns rolling a die and making that number of moves. For a move, you can choose to either move one of you pieces or the ball.

When moving a ball, choose a direction to slide along the board. The ball moves that way until obstructed by either a player or the side of the board. Below is an example.
