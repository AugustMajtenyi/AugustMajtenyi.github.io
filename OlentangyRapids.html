Olentangy Rapids

Overview: Olentangy Rapids is a game meant to physically engage students at OSU in a fun and challenging way.

Players: 1-4 main players

Estimated Play-Time: 3 minutes

Objective: Run around the stage and collect pieces to build your boat and escape the rushing water.


Rules

Project Members: Sarah Martin, Hannah Welling, Kai McKinney, and August Majtenyi

Process

Constraints: The game should be on OSU campus and incorporate projection mapping.

Most of the process work for this process exists on our Miro Page.

Visual Demonstrations

The visual demonstrations offers an understanding of what gameplay might be like for players on the stage as well as on the stairs.
